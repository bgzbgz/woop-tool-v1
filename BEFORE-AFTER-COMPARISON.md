### ‚ùå PROBLEMS:

1. **Incomplete Methodology**
   - Missing "How do I feel?" (Step 1)
   - Missing "Mental rehearsal" (Step 2)
   - Missing "Cut the elephant" (Step 4)
   - Only 2.5 of 4 WOOP steps present

2. **Corporate Jargon**
   - "Define specific, measurable outcomes"
   - "What tangible benefits do we expect"
   - "Describe a scenario in which..."
   - Fast Track CEOs tune this out immediately

3. **No Validation**
   - Static PDF = no feedback
   - No guidance on what's "good enough"
   - Can submit incomplete
   - No sense of progress

4. **Overwhelming Layout**
   - All questions visible at once
   - No clear structure
   - Table format is hard to fill
   - No visual hierarchy

5. **No Energy Creation**
   - Feels like homework
   - No gamification
   - No sense of achievement
   - Generic output (just filled text)

6. **Poor UX**
   - No auto-save
   - No real-time help
   - Can't share digitally
   - Print = messy margins
